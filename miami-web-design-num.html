Project Description

Comprehensive MedPsych Systems has been a client of ours for over 10 years. This is the latest upgrade to their website, to address their impressive growth with over 20 locations nationwide and counting.

Visitors to the website are able to search for staff members, or for a practice in their area via a module that provides maps, contact info and directions. They can view details on CMPS’services, policies and more. The website was created for optimal viewing experience across a wide range of devices and is search engine friendly.
